# Social Media & Outreach

## Platform Focus
*   **LinkedIn**: Primary for B2B. Connect with Procurement Managers, Health & Safety Officers.
*   **Facebook/Instagram**: Retargeting and brand awareness for the SME/Consumer segment.

## Content Strategy
*   **The "Helper" Content**: Tips on workplace safety, organization hacks.
*   **Social Proof**: Photos of our products in use by real customers.
*   **Company Culture**: Show the team. People buy from people.

## Scheduling
*   **Tools**: Use Buffer or Hootsuite.
*   **Cadence**:
    *   LinkedIn: 2x Week (Industry news, Case studies).
    *   FB/Insta: 3x Week (Product highlights, Reviews).
