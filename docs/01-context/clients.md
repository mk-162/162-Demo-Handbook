# Client Personas

## Target Audience

162 serves technology-forward organisations that recognise the potential of AI but struggle with inconsistent output and operational chaos.

---

## Primary Personas

### 1. The CTO (Chief Technology Officer)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35-50
- Location: London, UK (or remote UK/EU)
- Company Size: 50-500 employees
- Industry: SaaS, Fintech, E-commerce

**Pain Points:**
- Team is pasting the same context into ChatGPT repeatedly
- No audit trail of AI-generated content
- Concerned about data leakage and security
- Can't scale AI usage across the organisation

**Goals:**
- Standardise AI output quality
- Maintain security and compliance
- Reduce time spent on repetitive tasks
- Enable team self-service

**How 162 Helps:**
- Zero-Trust architecture addresses security concerns
- Version-controlled context ensures consistency
- Runbooks provide audit trail
- Scales across teams without chaos

**Quote:**
> "I need AI to be a team member, not a toy. 162 gives us the structure to make that real."

---

### 2. The Ops Manager

**Demographics:**
- Age: 28-40
- Location: UK/EU
- Company Size: 20-200 employees
- Industry: Agency, E-commerce, Professional Services

**Pain Points:**
- Spending hours each week on repetitive reporting
- Team uses different prompts for the same tasks
- Output quality varies wildly
- Can't delegate AI tasks to junior team members

**Goals:**
- Automate routine workflows
- Ensure consistent output quality
- Free up time for strategic work
- Train team on AI best practices

**How 162 Helps:**
- Runbooks standardise processes
- Agents can execute tasks without constant supervision
- Context ensures brand consistency
- Training gets team up to speed quickly

**Quote:**
> "I was spending 10 hours a week on reports. Now my agent does it in 10 minutes."

---

### 3. The Founder/CEO

**Demographics:**
- Age: 30-45
- Location: UK (primarily London)
- Company Size: 5-50 employees
- Industry: Startup, Agency, Consultancy

**Pain Points:**
- Wearing too many hats
- Can't afford a full AI team
- Needs to move fast but maintain quality
- Worried about technical debt

**Goals:**
- Leverage AI without hiring specialists
- Move faster than competitors
- Maintain quality as they scale
- Build durable systems

**How 162 Helps:**
- Managed Handbook = no technical overhead
- "Boring Technology" philosophy = no fragile dependencies
- Fast implementation = immediate ROI
- Scales with their growth

**Quote:**
> "162 lets us punch above our weight. We operate like a company 10x our size."

---

## Secondary Personas

### 4. The Marketing Director

- **Needs:** Content creation at scale
- **Pain Point:** Brand voice inconsistent across AI-generated content
- **162 Solution:** Brand context codified in immutable files

### 5. The Customer Success Lead

- **Needs:** Personalised responses at scale
- **Pain Point:** Can't trust AI with customer communications
- **162 Solution:** Runbooks with approval gates and context checks

## How to Speak to Each Persona

### CTO
- Lead with security architecture
- Mention SOC 2 and compliance
- Provide technical documentation
- Offer self-hosted option

### Ops Manager
- Lead with time savings
- Show before/after examples
- Focus on ease of use
- Offer training package

### Founder
- Lead with speed to value
- Emphasise "no technical overhead"
- Show ROI calculations
- Offer managed service

## Exclusions

We do NOT target:

- Companies seeking "AI magic" without process
- Organisations without defined workflows
- Teams unwilling to invest in context creation
- Individuals (we're B2B only)
