## How to Quote

1. **Understand needs** — Discovery call
2. **Recommend service** — Match to persona
3. **Customise scope** — Add/remove components
4. **Apply discounts** — Check eligibility
5. **Present proposal** — Clear deliverables and timeline
6. **Negotiate** — Within approved bounds

**Approval required for:**
- Discounts > 20%
- Custom scopes
- Payment terms > 30 days
- International travel
