# Marketing Strategy

## The 162 Go-to-Market Plan

## Positioning

**Category:** Operational AI Architecture

**Tagline:** "Stop Prompting. Start Operating."

**Positioning Statement:**
> For technology leaders who are frustrated with inconsistent AI output, 162 is an AI consultancy that turns scattered business processes into structured, executable knowledge. Unlike generic AI tools or prompt engineering services, we build durable, version-controlled Handbooks that make AI a reliable team member.

## Target Audience

### Primary

1. **CTOs** at 50-500 person companies
2. **Ops Managers** at 20-200 person companies
3. **Founders** at 5-50 person startups

### Secondary

4. Marketing Directors needing consistent brand voice
5. Customer Success Leads wanting to scale personalised support

## Content Pillars

### 1. The Problem (40% of content)

- Why generic AI fails
- The cost of operational chaos
- Security risks of current approaches

**Formats:** Opinion pieces, case studies, data reports

### 2. The Solution (30% of content)

- The 162 Methodology
- Handbook architecture
- Success stories

**Formats:** How-to guides, technical deep-dives, client stories

### 3. The Philosophy (20% of content)

- "Boring Technology"
- Durability over hype
- Knowledge management best practices

**Formats:** Manifestos, book reviews, industry commentary

### 4. The Company (10% of content)

- Team updates
- Behind-the-scenes
- Culture and values

**Formats:** Team intros, process shares, company news
