## Notes

### Executive Summary
- Keep to 2-3 sentences
- State the total value and key benefit
- Make it skimmable for decision-makers

### Understanding Needs Section
- Prove you listened
- Reference specific conversations or documents
- List requirements in their words, not yours

### Pricing Table Tips
- Include SKUs for easy reference
- Show unit price AND total
- Clearly mark what's included (delivery, VAT)
- Note any conditions or volume thresholds

### Terms to Always Include
- Payment terms
- Quote validity period
- Delivery terms and costs
- VAT treatment
- Returns policy

### Professional Formatting
- Use company letterhead or header
- Include quote reference number
- Date and expiry date prominent
- Contact details complete

### Next Steps
- Make action clear and simple
- Remove friction (attach forms, provide direct numbers)
- Create gentle urgency with validity period

### Common Mistakes
- Burying the price (put it prominently)
- Vague next steps ("let us know if interested")
- Missing validity date
- Inconsistent formatting
- Forgetting VAT notation
