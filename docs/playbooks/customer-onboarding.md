# Customer Onboarding Playbook

New B2B customer account setup from first contact to first order.

## Overview

This playbook covers the complete onboarding journey for new B2B customers. A well-executed onboarding builds trust, reduces churn, and sets the stage for long-term partnerships.

**Goal**: Convert signed B2B customer into an active, satisfied account within 30 days.

**Timeline**: 30 days from contract signature to check-in call.

**Owner**: Account Manager / Sales Team

---

## Prerequisites

- [ ] Signed customer agreement/contract
- [ ] Customer contact details (primary + accounts payable)
- [ ] BigCommerce admin access
- [ ] Email marketing platform access (Klaviyo/Mailchimp)
- [ ] Trade price list (current version)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Welcome Email Sequence (Day 0-1)

**Send within 24 hours of signing**

1. **Welcome Email** (Day 0)
   - Personal greeting from Account Manager
   - Confirm key details: Company name, primary contact
   - Attach: Trade Terms PDF, Key Contact List
   - Include: Account Manager direct line

2. **Account Setup Email** (Day 1)
   - BigCommerce login credentials
   - Quick start guide for placing orders
   - Link to product catalog (PDF or online)
   - Support contact information

3. **First Order Incentive** (Day 3)
   - Special offer on first order (e.g., free shipping, sample pack)
   - Time-limited to encourage action (expires in 14 days)

### Step 2: BigCommerce Account Setup (Day 0-2)

1. **Create Customer Account**
   - Navigate to Customers → Add Customer
   - Enter company details and primary contact
   - Assign to appropriate Customer Group

2. **Configure B2B Pricing Group**
   - Trade Pricing Group: Standard trade discount (typically 25-40% off RRP)
   - Volume Pricing Group: For high-volume customers (40%+ off RRP)
   - Custom Pricing: For negotiated rates (requires approval)

3. **Set Minimum Order Value**
   - Standard trade: £100 minimum
   - Premium accounts: No minimum (requires approval)

4. **Enable Account Features**
   - Purchase orders: Yes (if Net-30 approved)
   - Quote requests: Yes
   - Order history: Yes

### Step 3: Payment Terms Setup (Day 1-5)

1. **Credit Check** (if Net-30 requested)
   - Run credit check via Experian Business/Creditsafe
   - Review: Credit limit recommendation, payment history
   - Approve/deny based on risk threshold

2. **If Approved for Net-30:**
   - Set credit limit in BigCommerce
   - Configure payment terms (Net-30 from invoice date)
   - Add to accounts receivable tracking
   - Send confirmation email with terms

3. **If Proforma Required:**
   - Configure account for upfront payment only
   - Communicate clearly in welcome sequence
   - Offer review after 6 months of good payment history

### Step 4: Catalog & Price List Delivery (Day 2-3)

1. **Generate Trade Price List**
   - Export from BigCommerce with trade pricing
   - Format: PDF (printable) + Excel (for customer's system)
   - Include: SKUs, product names, trade prices, pack sizes

2. **Product Catalog**
   - Digital catalog link (website or Issuu/Flipbook)
   - Physical catalog if requested (note in CRM for next print run)

3. **Key Products Briefing**
   - Highlight bestsellers in their sector
   - Cross-sell opportunities
   - New products relevant to their business

### Step 5: First Order Support (Day 3-14)

1. **Proactive Check-in** (Day 5)
   - Call or email: "Any questions about placing your first order?"
   - Address any login issues, pricing questions
   - Offer to walk through the ordering process

2. **First Order Processing**
   - Flag first orders for priority handling
   - Personal QC check before dispatch
   - Include handwritten thank you note in shipment
   - Add product samples or promotional items

3. **First Order Follow-up** (Within 48 hours of delivery)
   - Confirm receipt and satisfaction
   - Ask: "Did everything arrive as expected?"
   - Document any feedback in CRM

### Step 6: 30-Day Check-in (Day 30)

1. **Schedule Call**
   - 15-30 minute relationship call
   - Review: First order experience, product feedback

2. **Discussion Topics**
   - How are our products performing vs competitors?
   - Any products you wish we stocked?
   - Are pricing and payment terms working?
   - Upcoming projects we can support?

3. **Document & Action**
   - Log feedback in CRM
   - Create tasks for any follow-ups
   - Schedule next quarterly touchpoint

---

## Checklist

Before marking onboarding complete:

- [ ] Welcome email sequence sent (3 emails)
- [ ] BigCommerce account created with correct pricing group
- [ ] Payment terms configured and communicated
- [ ] Trade price list delivered
- [ ] First order placed and delivered successfully
- [ ] 30-day check-in call completed
- [ ] Feedback documented in CRM
- [ ] Next quarterly touchpoint scheduled
