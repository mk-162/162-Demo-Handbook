# Marketing Skills

Skills for planning and executing GTSE's B2B marketing strategy, with focus on content and social media.

## Available Skills

### [Content Strategy](./content-strategy/SKILL)

**`gtse-content-strategy`**

B2B content strategy for GTSE industrial supplies. Plan content that drives organic traffic from trade customers, procurement managers, and facility managers.

**Use when:**
- Planning blog content
- Creating technical guides
- Developing content calendar
- Identifying content gaps

**Key features:**
- B2B industrial focus
- Technical content templates
- Keyword strategy framework
- Content calendar structure

---

### [Social Content](./social-content/SKILL)

**`gtse-social-content`**

B2B social media strategy with LinkedIn as primary focus. Create content that engages trade professionals and procurement managers.

**Use when:**
- Creating LinkedIn posts
- Planning social calendar
- Developing video content
- Building B2B engagement

**Key features:**
- LinkedIn-first strategy
- Product demo templates
- Industry tip formats
- B2B engagement tactics
