# Email Newsletter Strategy

We do not spam. We add value.

## The "Trade Talk" Weekly
*   **Audience**: B2B Customers.
*   **Content Mix**:
    *   **20%**: Product Promos (Flash Sale on Tape).
    *   **40%**: Industry News (Copper price changes, Shipping delays, Regulation updates).
    *   **40%**: Educational (Link to our latest Blog/Video).

## Flows (Automation)

### 1. The Welcome Series
*   **Email 1**: "Welcome to the Club" (Brand Story + 10% Code).
*   **Email 2**: "Who we serve" (Case studies of big clients).
*   **Email 3**: "Did you know?" (Highlighting our most popular but obscure products).

### 2. The Abandoned Cart (Aggressive)
*   **1 Hr**: "You left this behind."
*   **12 Hrs**: "Stock is low." (Scarcity).
*   **24 Hrs**: "Here is 5% off to finish the order."

### 3. The "Win Back" (Re-engagement)
*   **Trigger**: No purchase in 90 days.
*   **Subject**: "Have you retired?"
*   **Body**: "We haven't seen you in a while. Here is a $20 credit to restock your consumables."
