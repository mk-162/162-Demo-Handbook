# Trade Pricing & Credit Terms Strategy

To win B2B, we must think like a bank, not just a shop.

## The Trade Program ("GTSE PRO")

### Benefits
1.  **Tiered Pricing**:
    *   **Bronze**: Public pricing.
    *   **Silver** ($5k/yr spend): -10% off.
    *   **Gold** ($20k/yr spend): -20% off + Free Next Day Delivery.
    *   **Platinum** ($100k+): Negotiated Contract Pricing.
2.  **Credit Terms**:
    *   **Net-30**: Standard for established businesses.
    *   **Net-60**: For major construction firms (high volume, slow pay).
3.  **VAT/Tax Exemption**: Automated validation for EU/US tax IDs.

## Approval Process
*   **Risk Assessment**:
    *   Use a tool like CreditSafe or Dun & Bradstreet.
    *   *Skill*: "Run Credit Check" (Agent task).
*   **Limits**: Start small ($1k limit). Increase after 3 successful payments.

## Managing "Quotes"
BigCommerce allows "Request a Quote".
*   **SLA**: All quotes must be answered within 4 hours.
*   **Strategy**: Always round *down* the unit price to a psychological number.
    *   *List*: $0.14/unit.
    *   *Calc*: $0.12/unit.
    *   *Quote*: $0.115/unit (Show them you are sharpening the pencil).
