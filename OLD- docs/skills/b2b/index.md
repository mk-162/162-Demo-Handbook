# B2B Skills

Skills for acquiring and converting trade customers, procurement managers, and bulk buyers.

## Available Skills

### [Cold Outreach Generator](./cold-outreach/SKILL)

**`gtse-cold-outreach-generator`**

Generate personalized cold outreach messages for GTSE B2B sales. Create emails, LinkedIn messages, and call scripts for trade customer acquisition.

**Use when:**
- Reaching new trade prospects
- Creating email campaigns
- Drafting LinkedIn outreach
- Preparing sales call scripts

**Key features:**
- Industry-specific templates
- Segment-based messaging
- Email, LinkedIn, and phone templates
- Personalization framework

---

### [Outreach Sequencer](./outreach-sequencer/SKILL)

**`gtse-outreach-sequencer`**

Design multi-touch follow-up sequences for trade customer conversion. Includes timing, messaging, and touchpoint strategies.

**Use when:**
- Building follow-up sequences
- Planning multi-channel outreach
- Creating quote follow-ups
- Reactivating lapsed customers

**Key features:**
- Pre-built sequence templates
- Optimal timing guidelines
- Multi-channel orchestration
- Segment-specific approaches
