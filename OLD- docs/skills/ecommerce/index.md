# Ecommerce Skills

Skills for optimizing GTSE's ecommerce presence, SEO performance, and competitive positioning.

## Available Skills

### [SEO Audit](./seo-audit/SKILL)

**`gtse-ecommerce-seo-audit`**

Comprehensive BigCommerce SEO audit tool for product pages, collection pages, and technical SEO. Adapted for B2B industrial supplies with trade customer focus.

**Use when:**
- Auditing product page SEO
- Checking category/collection optimization
- Analyzing technical SEO issues
- BigCommerce-specific SEO review

**Key features:**
- BigCommerce-specific checks
- B2B keyword strategy
- Internal linking analysis
- Product schema validation

---

### [Competitor Analyzer](./competitor-analyzer/SKILL)

**`gtse-competitor-analyzer`**

B2B industrial supplies competitor analysis for GTSE. Analyze RS Components, Screwfix, Cromwell, and specialist suppliers.

**Use when:**
- Researching competitor pricing
- Analyzing competitor product ranges
- Identifying market gaps
- Planning competitive positioning

**Key features:**
- UK industrial supplies market focus
- Pricing intelligence framework
- SEO/content gap analysis
- Feature parity comparison
