# B2B Platform Strategy for GTSE

Platform-specific strategies adapted for industrial supplies B2B marketing.

## LinkedIn (Primary Platform)

**Best for:** Trade professionals, procurement managers, facility managers
**Audience:** Decision-makers, B2B buyers, industry professionals
**Posting frequency:** 3-4x per week
**Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am, 12-1pm, 4-5pm

**What works for GTSE:**
- Product demonstrations and applications
- Industry tips and best practices
- Customer success stories
- Behind-the-scenes (warehouse, quality control)
- Technical expertise posts
- Compliance and regulation updates

**What doesn't work:**
- Overly promotional "buy now" posts
- Generic motivational content
- External links in post body (kills reach)
- Corporate jargon without personality

**Format tips:**
- First line is everything (hook before "see more")
- Use line breaks for readability
- 1,200-1,500 characters performs well
- Put links in comments, not post body
- Document posts (carousels) get strong reach
- Tag customers (with permission) for social proof

**GTSE-specific content ideas:**
- "Why stainless steel cable ties outperform nylon in outdoor use"
- "3 PPE mistakes we see electricians make"
- "How [customer type] saved 20% on supplies"
- "Cable tie tensile strength comparison" (video)
- "What EN standards actually mean for hi-vis clothing"

## Facebook (Secondary Platform)

**Best for:** Trade communities, local business reach, customer reviews
**Audience:** Trade professionals, small business owners
**Posting frequency:** 2-3x per week
**Best times:** 12-4pm weekdays

**What works for GTSE:**
- Product tips and hacks
- Special offers and promotions
- Customer photos and reviews
- Quick how-to videos
- Trade event coverage

**What doesn't work:**
- External links (reach killer)
- Long-form content (save for blog)
- Ignoring comments and reviews
- Cross-posting LinkedIn content without adaptation

**GTSE-specific content ideas:**
- "Quick tip: Choosing the right cable tie for your job"
- Customer photo: "Great to see our products on site!"
- "New product alert: Heavy duty stainless steel range"
- "Behind the scenes at GTSE warehouse"

---

## YouTube (Supporting Platform)

**Best for:** Product demos, tutorials, long-form educational content
**Audience:** Trade professionals researching products
**Publishing frequency:** 2-4x per month
**SEO value:** High (YouTube is 2nd largest search engine)

**What works for GTSE:**
- Product demonstration videos (1-3 minutes)
- "How to choose" guides (5-10 minutes)
- Installation tutorials
- Side-by-side comparisons
- Application-specific guides

**Video types to create:**
1. **Product Demos** (1-2 min)
   - "Heavy Duty Cable Ties - Tensile Strength Test"
   - "Cable Tie Gun Tutorial"
   
2. **Comparison Videos** (3-5 min)
   - "Nylon vs Stainless Steel Cable Ties"
   - "Releasable vs Standard Cable Ties"

3. **Educational Content** (5-10 min)
   - "Complete Guide to Cable Tie Selection"
   - "PPE Compliance for Construction Sites"

4. **Behind the Scenes** (2-3 min)
   - "How We Pick and Pack Your Orders"
   - "Quality Control at GTSE"
