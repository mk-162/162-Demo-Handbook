## How to Use Playbooks

1. **Read the Overview** ‚Äî Understand the goal and scope
2. **Customize** ‚Äî Fill in `[CUSTOMIZE]` sections with your specifics
3. **Check Prerequisites** ‚Äî Ensure you have access/tools needed
4. **Follow Steps** ‚Äî Execute in order, checking off as you go
5. **Reference Related** ‚Äî Links to supporting docs and skills

## Customization Guide

Each playbook contains `[CUSTOMIZE]` markers for:
- **Company specifics** (team names, roles, contacts)
- **Metrics targets** (revenue, conversion rates, etc.)
- **Pricing/terms** (margins, credit limits, discounts)
- **Process details** (tools, systems, workflows)
- **Templates** (email copy, scripts, proposals)

Complete the customization checklist at the bottom of each playbook before rolling out to your team.
